Phase One

Define high-value patient profiles. Week 1

Before scaling, we sharpen the aim. Week 1 is dedicated to refining who exactly TCT is acquiring, why current website traffic is sliding, and what's behind the lead-generation bottleneck. Targeting precision is the foundation everything else compounds on.

What We Do (Silk Road)

  • Audit current patient data and identify the highest-LTV profiles
  • Diagnose the website traffic decrease and pinpoint root causes
  • Map the existing lead-generation funnel and flag every drop-off
  • Refine ideal patient targeting — demographics, intent signals, geography

Phase 1 Outcome

  • Validated patient personas tied to highest revenue per patient
  • Documented root cause of the website traffic decline
  • Lead-gen bottleneck identified with a clear fix path
  • Targeting brief approved by Augie — ready for build
02
Phase Two

Build your patient acquisition engine. Weeks 2 – 3

With targeting locked, we move into construction. Two weeks to assemble the multi-channel campaign infrastructure, solve the lead-generation bottleneck identified in Phase 1, and prepare every channel for synchronized launch.

What We Do (Silk Road)

  • Build new multi-channel campaigns — paid search, social, email, SMS
  • Design landing-page experiences engineered for conversion
  • Solve the lead-generation bottleneck with a redesigned intake flow
  • Connect every channel into a unified attribution dashboard

Phase 2 Outcome

  • Multi-channel campaign infrastructure ready for activation
  • Conversion-optimized landing pages live in staging
  • Lead-gen bottleneck resolved — new intake flow tested end-to-end
  • Unified dashboard accessible for both teams
03

Launch campaigns & fill the pipeline. Weeks 4 – 6

Activation. Three weeks to move TCT from 15 new patients per month toward the 30+ target — and prove the engine works at the volume needed to reach the 40–60 monthly scale goal.

  • Drive new patient acquisition from 15 toward the 40–60/month range
  • Weekly performance reviews with TCT leadership

Phase 3 Outcome

  • Monthly new-patient count crossing the 30+ threshold
  • Validated cost-per-acquisition figures across every channel
  • Clear visibility into which campaigns scale and which retire
  • Pipeline depth confirmed for Phase 4 scaling

  • Report on every metric tied to TCT's valuation milestones

Phase 4 Outcome
